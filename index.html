About The Church of Pentecost

The Church of Pentecost is a global religious organisation with its International Headquarters in Accra, Ghana. Led by a dedicated team of Apostles, we are committed to our "Vision 2028" and an annual theme that directs our mission of spiritual growth and societal transformation.

We are a vibrant community that supports the holistic development of our members, celebrating achievements in faith, education, and technology. Through events, crusades, and active digital ministries on all major social platforms, we strive to spread the gospel and nurture believers.

The Vision of the Church

The Church of Pentecost is a vibrant, worldwide Christian fellowship. Guided by our "Vision 2028: Possessing the Nations," we are dedicated to equipping our members to become agents of positive change, spiritually impacting their communities and every aspect of society.

Our Vision Statement

The Mission of the Church

Our mission is to build a spirit-filled community where every member is nurtured into Christ-like maturity through the teaching of the Word, prayer, and fellowship. We are called to equip and empower believers to become agents of positive change, transforming every sphere of society with the principles and values of God's kingdom

Our Foundation

Core Values

Guided by biblical principles, our core values shape our identity and direct our mission.

Pentecostal Faith

We embrace the fullness of the Holy Spirit, including the practice of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts for the edification of the church.

Biblical Holiness

We uphold biblical standards of holiness and modesty, living lives that reflect Christ's character and teachings in all aspects of daily living.

Communal Living

We practice communal living, supporting one another spiritually, emotionally, and materially as members of God's family.

Self-Support

We are a self-supporting church, sustained by the faithful giving of tithes and offerings from our members.

Moral Purity

We maintain strict biblical standards, abstaining from alcohol, drugs, and all forms of sexual immorality, including homosexuality.

Leadership Development

We are committed to developing leaders from within our membership, equipping them to serve and lead according to biblical principles.
